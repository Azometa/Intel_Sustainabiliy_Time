Intel Technology Timeline

1968

Intel Founded

The beginning of semiconductor innovation.

Intel’s founding marked the start of modern microelectronics and paved the way for energy-efficient computing.
1971

First Microprocessor

Computing becomes smaller and more efficient.

The microprocessor revolutionized computing by putting powerful processing into tiny, efficient chips.
1978

8086 Architecture

Architecture improvements drive performance.

Better chip designs improved performance while reducing energy demands.
1993

Pentium Era

Personal computing becomes mainstream.

With computers in homes and offices, energy efficiency became increasingly important.
2006

Efficiency Focus

Performance-per-watt becomes key.

Processors were redesigned to deliver more computing power with less electricity.
2013

Water & Waste Initiatives

Sustainability expands to operations.

Intel began focusing heavily on reducing water use and manufacturing waste.
2020

Renewable Energy

Clean energy powers technology.

Data centers and manufacturing increasingly rely on renewable energy sources.
2030

Future Goals

Ambitious sustainability targets.

Intel commits to major reductions in emissions and resource usage.
2040

Net-Zero Vision

A carbon-neutral future.

Long-term goals focus on eliminating environmental impact entirely.
